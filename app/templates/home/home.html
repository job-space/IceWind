History of ice cream

Even the legendary doctor Hippocrates, who is called the father of medicine, advised eating, as he called it, "frozen cream", which, in his opinion, improves well-being. By the way, the world-famous commander Alexander the Great was a great lover of ice cream. A sweet mixture was prepared for him - various fruits with honey, and then it was cooled and frozen on ice. In Ancient Rome, snow and ice were placed in specially equipped rooms. In the summer, this snow was mixed with honey and cheese and served to the table of rich people. But even Roman ice cream, it turns out, is not the oldest. There is information that it was known in the countries of the Far East a thousand years before our era. It was from China that the traveler and merchant Marco Polo brought the secret of making fruit ice cream to Spain in the 18th century. Since then, this original dish has become fashionable in Europe. But it was only fruit ice cream. And a delicacy made of milk and cream was invented by the cook of the French king when he was at the court of the English king. For a long time, ice cream was a privilege of the rich, poor people knew nothing about it.
