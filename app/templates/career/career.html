Our HR policy:

One of the main indicators of the quality and success of the institution is a good team of employees. In the company "Ice-wind", we pay special attention to the selection of the right personnel. It is such people who will be able to make the client happy, and this is one of the main missions of our institution. In our company, everyone can grow and develop. You can start from the position of administrator of the institution, show yourself by showing your responsibility and initiative. But, expanding our network, we can offer you a position, for example, a regional manager. We have ambitious plans to increase our network to 100 establishments in the next few months, so you will definitely find a good place to work!



Our values

Quality in everything is our first and foremost value. Our employees are the main capital of the company. Each of us treats the company as our own business. Our Guest always receives a quality product and a good mood. We know how to set correct and clear goals and, of course, achieve them. We are honest and fair in everything and to everyone - guests, colleagues and partners and first of all to ourselves. Cleanliness and order - everywhere and in everything. We are committed to long-term relationships. We think creatively and out of the box. We provide continuity through mentorship and belief in everyone. We are sincere and positive. The development of the company and the professional development of everyone go in unison and rapidly uphill.



Mission

Our mission is big and clear at the same time - to make everyone's everyday life more comfortable and efficient thanks to fast, high-quality and safe food preparation. Guests of "Ice-wind" value their time and aim to get only the best, the "Ice-wind" team helps them in this by creating a positive atmosphere: pleasant and sincere communication, high-quality dishes prepared as quickly as possible, and of course - an individual approach!
